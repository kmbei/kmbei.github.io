52 West 14th Street Development
 Conversion from commercial  to  mixed-use buildling

Location: New York, New York
Area: 15539 sf
Client: 52 W 14th Street LLC
Status: Completed 2004

This is a complete renovation of a previous commercial building. Three additional floors were added to become 7 storeys mixed-use building. The use and occupancy of this building was upgraded from 4 commercial stores/office spaces to 18 apartment units on upper floors with new commercial retail space on ground floor. The new building was also transformed from wood joist to concrete/steel beam structure. Elevator is a complement to this new building.
