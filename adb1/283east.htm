283 East 7 Street Development
Complete gut renovation on mixed-use building

Location: New York, New York
Area: 16,863 sf
Client: Seven D, LLC
Status: Completed 2001

This project involved redesign and renovation of the previous unsafe and vacant building. Occupancy was substantially improved to this old building through the design and construction to accommodate 23 new apartment units. New commercial retail space is occupied on part of ground floor.
