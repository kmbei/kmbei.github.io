The Salvation Army
Design Proposal  of the conversion from office to mixed-use building

Total floor area: 49,998 sf 
Estimated construction budget: 10 million
Location: 132 West 14th Street, New York, NY
Client: The Salvation Army

 

 

  ADB is involved with the development of zoning analysis, initial planning and numerous schematic designs for this new proposed 10 storeys building renovation.


This existing seven storeys office building is currently occupied with the community center on first three floors. ADB met with Corps Officers and users of the spaces, redesigned the community center and provided the Corps with new operational efficient layouts and flexible as well as multiple use of space.


The existing offices located on the fourth through seventh floors are being converted into residential apartments for Corps’ officers. An addition of three floors is being constructed to provide additional residential spaces.
