100 Delancey Street Development
Redevelopment of 6 storey mixed-use
building

Location: New York, New York
Area: 30, 751 s.f.
Client: Escava Brothers
Status: Construction is in progress

 

 

 

 

 

This building is situated at the busy intersection of Delancey
and Ludlow streets along a major artery (1 minute from
Williamsburg Bridge) in a historical area of lower east side
Manhattan. ADB is acting as project manager for the
redevelopment which will transform the building into an
elegant architectural identity in the neighbourhood. The
building program includes retrofitted retail space on basement
and ground floor, new fitness center for adults on 2nd floor, two
new floors will be added on the upper floors to existing
building to accommodate 21 new luxurious apartments.
