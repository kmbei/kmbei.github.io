About Us

ADB Associates is a Design-Build and Green Building Consulting firm headquartered in New York City founded in 2002. ADB provides integrated architectural, multi-disciplined engineering and construction management service to the residential, commercial, institutional, and mixed-use commercial/residential properties.

ADB also provides expediting expertise in all areas of Buildings Department, City Planning Commission and Landmarks Preservation Commission filings, approvals and permits for the energy efficiency retrofits and solar related projects.

ADB employs standards of energy efficiency, green building practices, standard and quality of construction.
